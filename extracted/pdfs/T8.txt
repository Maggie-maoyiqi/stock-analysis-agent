1 Review
1. The main objective of the classification model:
To determine whether the treatment groups have any systematic effects on the response variable,
and to examine the nature of these effects.
Technically, our underlying model treats all observations belonging to the same treatment group
as a random sample from the same normal distribution. The idea is to study how the means of
all these normal distributions vary over the different treatment groups.
